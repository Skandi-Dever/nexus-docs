# Nexus Management System

**The Hybrid-Decentralized Enterprise Management Platform**

Welcome to the official homepage for the Nexus Management System. Nexus is a desktop-grade, enterprise management application designed to give organizations absolute control over their operational data, member records, and automated workflows.

### What is Nexus?
Nexus is built on a "Double-Brain" architecture. It combines the speed of local JavaFX desktop clients with the resilience of cloud-based synchronization. It is designed to handle:
* **Member Management:** Securely process and store personnel and registration data.
* **Automated Operations:** Seamlessly integrate with Google Workspace to automate event scheduling, file storage, and data capture.
* **Data Sovereignty:** Choose between Self-Hosted (On-Premise) or Nexus-Hosted (Cloud) database modalities to meet strict POPIA compliance requirements.

### Google Workspace Integration
Nexus utilizes secure Google OAuth 2.0 to automate administrative workflows. By integrating with Google Drive, Calendar, Forms, and Gmail, Nexus eliminates repetitive data entry while ensuring your organization's files remain securely within your own Google Workspace.
