# Nexus Management System

**Connecting your community, simplifying your workflow.**  
*Produced and Engineered by Kernel 7 Group (Pty) Ltd.*

Welcome to the official homepage for the Nexus Management System. Whether you are managing a church, a youth centre, or a business, Nexus provides a completely private and isolated digital home for your organization. 

### Core Modules
Nexus simplifies your workflow with powerful, intuitive tools designed for comprehensive management:
* **Structure & Member Management:** Maintain a secure member directory. Streamline onboarding with our Remote Registration system, allowing new members to sign up via Google Forms and land directly in your system's Waiting List for approval.
* **Events & Scheduling:** Stay organized with an integrated timeline that automatically synchronizes with Google Calendar.
* **Inventory Tracking:** Monitor your organization's assets, track equipment conditions, and manage supplies effortlessly.

### The "Double-Brain" Architecture
Nexus is built on an advanced dual-database architecture utilizing both MariaDB and MongoDB to ensure speed, security, and flexibility. You retain absolute data sovereignty by choosing between two hosting modalities:
* **Self-Hosted (Private):** Host your data on your own servers or chosen third-party infrastructure for complete internal control.
* **Nexus-Hosted (Cloud):** Utilize our secure, fully managed database clusters maintained by Kernel 7 Group.

### Google Workspace Integration
Nexus utilizes secure Google OAuth 2.0 to automate administrative workflows. By seamlessly integrating with Google Drive, Calendar, Forms, and Gmail, Nexus eliminates repetitive data entry while ensuring your organization's files remain securely within your own Google Workspace ecosystem.
